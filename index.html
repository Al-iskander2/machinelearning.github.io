Stock Predictor

Function: Anticipates the rise or fall of any listed stock price.
Use: Simply enter the stock "ticker" in the notebook to get a prediction based on an LSTM model that analyzes historical time series, generates technical indicators (e.g., moving averages, RSI), and returns the probability of an upward or downward trend.

ML-Encryption

Function: Creates encryption layers for sensitive data using an autoencoder trained with TensorFlow.
Use: Generates cryptograms that can only be decrypted with the correct key, comparing its strength to conventional encryption systems; ideal for securing information in transit or at rest using privacy-preserving ML techniques.

ML-PDF-Scan

Function: Analyzes PDF files to detect whether they contain malicious content (hidden scripts, dangerous links) or are safe.
Use: Uses OCR (Tesseract) and OpenCV filters to extract and process text and images, then a trained classifier determines the PDF's nature; useful for automatic scanning of large volumes of documents.

ML-View

Function: Recognizes the content of images using a convolutional neural network trained with CIFAR-10.
Use: Select one of three sample images (cat, car, dog) and let the model predict what's in it. It shows the confidence score and a visual result; ideal for demonstrating how image recognition works with deep learning.
